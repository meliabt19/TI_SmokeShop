About Us

Based in central North Carolina, The Island is committed to providing a premium smoke shop experience. That commitment starts with you - our guest. Our shelves are restocked weekly with all of your favorite brands like RAW, Fume, King Palm & More! With new products being added frequently, you’re bound to find a perfect match to fit your smoking needs. Each TI location has a dedicated lounging area open 24/7. Hookahs are fully prepared by our staff, and can be enjoyed inside and seasonally on our outside lounging areas. Flat screen TVs are also available for guest use. Stop by ANY Location, ANYtime for a paradise unlike any other.
